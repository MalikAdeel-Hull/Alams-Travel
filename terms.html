Terms & Conditions

Please read these terms carefully before making a booking with Alams Travel.

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Alams Travel, Derby

1. About Us

These terms and conditions govern all bookings made with Alams Travel, a private hire and minibus operator based in Derby, England. By requesting or confirming a booking with us — whether by phone, WhatsApp, email, or our website — you confirm that you have read, understood and agreed to these terms.

Alams Travel operates under a valid Private Hire Operator's Licence issued by Derby City Council, and holds a Public Service Vehicle (PSV) Operator's Licence for vehicles with more than eight passenger seats. Full licensing and insurance details are provided with every quotation.

2. Bookings and Confirmation

A booking is not confirmed until Alams Travel has provided you with a written or verbal confirmation, along with an agreed price. Verbal confirmations are followed up in writing (by email, SMS or WhatsApp) wherever possible.

It is your responsibility to check that all details in your booking confirmation — including the date, time, pickup address and destination — are accurate. Please notify us immediately of any errors.

Alams Travel reserves the right to decline any booking at its absolute discretion.

3. Pricing and Quotes

All prices quoted by Alams Travel are fixed and all-inclusive of driver time, fuel and standard waiting time unless stated otherwise in your quotation. There are no hidden charges.

Prices shown on our website are starting estimates only. Your confirmed, fixed price is agreed with our team before any booking is locked in — we will never charge you more than the price agreed at confirmation.

The following surcharges may apply in certain circumstances:

  • Unsocial hours (midnight–6am): a surcharge may apply — this will be included in your quoted price.
  • UK Public Holidays: a surcharge of up to 50% may apply.
  • Christmas Day, Boxing Day, New Year's Day: a surcharge of up to 100% may apply.
  • Additional miles or route changes: if you request a longer route than agreed at booking, additional mileage charges may apply at our standard rate.

4. Payment

Payment is due on the day of travel unless a different arrangement has been agreed in writing. We accept cash and bank transfer. Corporate accounts with approved credit terms are invoiced and payable within 14 days of the invoice date.

No deposit is required to secure a booking unless otherwise agreed. Gratuities are entirely at the passenger's discretion and are not included in our charges.

5. Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is as follows, based on notice given before the scheduled pickup time:

  • More than 7 days' notice: No charge.
  • 3–7 days' notice: 25% of the agreed hire charge.
  • 24–72 hours' notice: 50% of the agreed hire charge.
  • Less than 24 hours' notice: 100% of the agreed hire charge.

Any overnight accommodation or other expenses already incurred by Alams Travel on your behalf at the time of cancellation will also be chargeable.

To cancel a booking, please contact us as soon as possible by phone on +44 7777 184601 or by WhatsApp on 07777 399135.

6. Punctuality and No-Shows

Our drivers will always aim to arrive at the agreed pickup time. If a passenger does not appear or make themselves known to the driver within 30 minutes of the scheduled pickup time, the driver may be stood down. The full hire charge will remain payable in this event.

For airport collections, the first 30 minutes of waiting time (calculated from the last confirmed landing time) is provided free of charge. Waiting time beyond this is chargeable.

7. Delays and Circumstances Beyond Our Control

Alams Travel will always endeavour to ensure on-time arrivals; however, we cannot accept liability for delays caused by vehicle breakdown, road traffic accidents, severe weather, road closures, diversions, or other circumstances outside our reasonable control.

In the event of a breakdown, we will make every effort to arrange alternative transport to get you to your destination.

We do not accept responsibility for missed flights, train connections or other onward travel caused by such delays. We strongly recommend allowing adequate time in your journey plan and purchasing travel insurance where appropriate.

8. Passenger Conduct

Our driver may, at their absolute discretion, refuse to carry any passenger who is considered to be a nuisance, is under the influence of alcohol or drugs to a degree that may cause a risk, or whose behaviour poses a threat to the driver, the vehicle or other passengers. In such circumstances, the full hire charge remains payable.

Smoking and the use of e-cigarettes are strictly prohibited in all Alams Travel vehicles, in accordance with UK law.

Passengers are responsible for any damage they cause to the interior or exterior of a vehicle. A soiling charge of up to £250 may be applied to cover valeting, repair and any loss of earnings while the vehicle is out of service.

9. Luggage and Property

Alams Travel accepts no responsibility for loss or damage to any luggage or property carried in or on our vehicles, however such loss or damage is caused. Passengers are advised to ensure their possessions are adequately insured.

Our driver retains the right to refuse carriage of excessive, heavy or sharp-edged luggage that may damage the vehicle or endanger passengers. Hazardous or illegal items will not be carried under any circumstances.

10. Subcontracting

Alams Travel may, on occasion, engage the services of associate drivers or partner companies to fulfil a booking, particularly for larger or specialist vehicles. All such partners are vetted by us and hold appropriate licences and insurance. These terms apply equally to all journeys fulfilled on our behalf.

11. Child Seats and Accessibility

If you require a child seat, booster seat or accessibility assistance, please inform us at the time of booking so we can make the necessary arrangements. We will always do our best to accommodate your needs.

12. Route

Our driver will travel by the most appropriate route on the day, having regard to traffic, road conditions and the agreed pickup time. If you wish the driver to use a specific route, please inform us at the time of booking. Additional charges may apply if the requested route is materially longer than our standard route.

13. Vehicle Changes

Alams Travel reserves the right to substitute a different vehicle or driver at any time if deemed necessary, provided the replacement vehicle is of at least equivalent capacity and comfort.

14. Governing Law

These terms and conditions are governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any dispute arising from a booking with Alams Travel will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts.
